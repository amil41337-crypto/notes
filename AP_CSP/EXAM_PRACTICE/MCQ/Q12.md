**Research**

**Constant Increasing rate overview**

If the relationship of a table has a constant increasing rate, that means that every single time the X increases the Y is multiplied by a constant rate and would increase by the same amount each time.

**Key Benefits**

A key benefit would be, being able to predict the Y at a given X at certain times in the table, which increases calculation, speed, and understanding if the rate is constant at all times.

**Implementation Examples**

For example, let's say there is a wildfire, and the fire is increasing at a constant rate it will help the firefighters and their teams to predict the length of the fire and how to attack the fire as it is increasing. And will also tell them if the fire is increasing at a high or low rate, which will also help them put out the fire.
