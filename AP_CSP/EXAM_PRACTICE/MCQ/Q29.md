**Diagram with three logic gates**

Similar to functions the 3 logic gates have relationships between their inputs and outputs within themselves and with each other. The inputs produce outputs at each gate, then both outputs of the gates are inputs of the final gate which produces the final output. 

**Key Benefits**

Knowing the relationships between the inputs and outputs lets individuals control the relationship to meet their standard and also experimenting with them in different ways to see the outputs and what input is given to the function.
